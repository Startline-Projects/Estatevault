# EstateVault — Partner Program Overview

*A simple guide to understanding our three partner types. Written for non-technical readers.*

---

## What Is EstateVault?

EstateVault helps everyday families protect their important life information — wills, trusts, insurance, financial accounts, digital passwords, contacts, and even personal farewell messages. Everything lives in one secure, online "vault" that loved ones can access when needed.

We work with professionals (financial advisors, accountants, estate planners, attorneys, insurance agents) who offer EstateVault to their own clients under their own brand. We call these professionals **Partners**.

There are three kinds of Partners: **Basic**, **Standard**, and **Enterprise**. Each one fits a different size of business and a different goal.

---

## 1. The Basic Partner

**Best for:** Small professionals who want to offer a simple, secure digital vault to their clients without getting into legal documents.

**What they pay us:** A one-time setup fee of **$500**.

### What They Get

- A **branded online vault** with their own name, logo, colors, and web address (for example: *yourname.estatevault.us*).
- A safe place where their clients can store:
  - Estate documents
  - Insurance policies
  - Financial account information
  - Digital passwords and logins
  - Important contacts
  - A personal farewell video for loved ones
- **Trustee access** — the client can choose 1 or 2 trusted people who can request access to the vault in an emergency. There's a built-in waiting period and identity check to keep things safe.
- A **PIN-locked vault** so only the right people can open it.
- A clean dashboard showing their vault clients and earnings.
- Built-in marketing materials to promote the vault to their clients.

### How They Earn Money

- Their clients pay **$99 per year** to use the vault.
- The Basic Partner keeps **Percentacge Assigned By Estate Vault**. 

### What They Don't Do

- Basic Partners do **not** create wills or trusts.
- They do **not** manage referrals or commissions on legal documents.
- They do **not** have a team — it's a solo operation.

### Quick Picture
Think of a Basic Partner as someone running a "digital safety deposit box" service for their clients, fully branded as their own business.

---

## 2. The Standard Partner

**Best for:** Growing professionals who want to offer real estate planning — wills, trusts, and the vault — to their clients as a complete service.

**What they pay us:** A one-time setup fee of **$1,200**.

### What They Get

Everything the Basic Partner gets, **plus a whole lot more:**

- A **full white-label estate planning platform** under their own brand and web address.
- Ability to help clients create:
  - **Wills** (price to client: $400)
  - **Trusts** (price to client: $600)
  - **Amendments** (price to client: $50)
- **Up to 3 team members** can log in and help run the business.
- A complete **client management system** — invite clients, start sessions, track progress.
- A **document order tracker** so they can see every client's status (pending, paid, in review, delivered).
- A **referral program** — when they refer clients to attorneys for review, they earn fees.
- An **attorney review add-on** — clients can pay an extra $300 to have a lawyer review their documents.
- A full **marketing toolkit**: email templates, social media posts, sales scripts, print materials.
- **Required training and certification** — 4 short training modules (about 3.5 hours total) so they know how to use everything properly. Includes a final test.
- A weekly automatic payout to their bank account every Friday.

### How They Earn Money

For every client they help:
- **$300 for each will**
- **$400 for each trust**
- **$35 for each amendment**
- **$75 every time a referral converts**
- Plus the **$99/year vault subscription** revenue from clients.

### Quick Picture
A Standard Partner runs a full estate planning office under their own brand, with EstateVault doing all the heavy lifting behind the scenes.

---

## 3. The Enterprise Partner

**Best for:** Large firms, big agencies, or established professionals who want top earnings, more control, and premium support.

**What they pay us:** A one-time setup fee of **$6,000**.

### What They Get

Everything the Standard Partner gets, **upgraded to the highest level:**

- **Higher earnings on every sale** (more on this below).
- **A custom web address** — they can use their own company domain (like *legacy.theirfirm.com*) instead of a subdomain of ours.
- **Up to 10 team members** can log in and work together.
- A **dedicated account manager** — a real person from EstateVault assigned to help them succeed.
- **Free attorney reviews** — they get **10 free attorney reviews per month** (Standard Partners pay for each one).
- **Sub-agent commission system** — they can build their own network of agents under them and pay commissions through the platform automatically.
- **Priority support** — front-of-line help whenever they need it.
- All the same training, marketing toolkit, and weekly payouts.

### How They Earn Money

Enterprise Partners earn more on every transaction:
- **$350 for each will** (vs. $300 for Standard)
- **$450 for each trust** (vs. $400 for Standard)
- **$40 for each amendment**
- **$2,250 for premium scenario packages**
- Plus referral fees, attorney review revenue, and vault subscription revenue.

### Quick Picture
An Enterprise Partner runs the biggest, most profitable version of the business — with their own team, their own custom web address, their own sub-agents, and a personal contact at EstateVault.
